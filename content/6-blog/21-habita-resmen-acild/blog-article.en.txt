Title: habita is formally opened!

----

Date: 2016-10-06

----

Text: 

(image: 5-ekim.png class: medium)

We had already started hosting the habitans since September, after the project and construction process. After completing rest of the operational stuff, the time for grand opening has come.

After one year of hard work, habita proudly announced it's grand opening! At 5th of October, we hosted Arda Kutsal, founder & CEO of Webrazzi and Erden Timur, founder of NEF. Our guests enjoyed their discussion on entrepreneurship and the after party.

(image: habita-5-ekim-14.png class: medium)

We thank all our friends, who supported us since the beginning of the habita dream and to our growing friends network at habita for being with us at this happy day!
